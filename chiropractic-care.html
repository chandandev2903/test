What Is Chiropractic Care ?

Chiropractic care is a hands-on therapy that primarily focuses on the alignment of the spine and musculoskeletal system. When the spine or joints are misaligned, it can lead to pain, stiffness, nerve compression, reduced mobility, and poor posture.
A chiropractor uses manual adjustments, mobilisation techniques, and supportive therapies to correct these misalignments, allowing the body to heal naturally without medications or surgery.

When Do You Need Chiropractic Care ?

You may benefit from chiropractic treatment if you experience

  • Chronic or recurring back pain
  • Neck pain or stiffness
  • Slip disc or disc bulge
  • Sciatica (leg pain due to nerve compression)
  • Shoulder, knee, or joint pain
  • Poor posture or spinal imbalance
  • Headaches or migraines linked to neck strain
  • Sports injuries or work-related strain
  • Restricted movement or stiffness
  • Post-injury or post-accident pain

Chiropractic care is also helpful for people looking for drug-free pain management and long-term spine health.

Frequently Asked Questions

is chiropractic treatment safe ?

Yes. Chiropractic care is safe when performed by trained professionals. Treatments are customised based on the patient’s condition and comfort level.

Does chiropractic treatment cause pain ?

Most patients experience little to no pain. Some may feel mild soreness initially, which usually settles within a day or two.

How many sessions will I need ?

The number of sessions depends on your condition, severity, and response to treatment. Some patients feel relief within a few sessions, while chronic conditions may need ongoing care.

Can chiropractic care treat slipped discs?

Yes. Chiropractic care can help manage slipped disc symptoms by reducing nerve pressure, improving alignment, and relieving pain—without surgery in many cases.

Is chiropractic care suitable for elderly patients ?

Yes. Gentle techniques are used for elderly patients to improve mobility, balance, and pain relief safely.

Can chiropractic care be combined with other treatments?

Yes. Chiropractic care works well alongside acupuncture, physiotherapy, and nutrition support when needed.
