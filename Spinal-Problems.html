What Are Spinal Problems?

Spinal problems occur when the bones, discs, nerves, or muscles of the spine are affected. Common causes include poor posture, long working hours, heavy physical activity, injuries, ageing, or degeneration of spinal discs.

If left untreated, spinal issues can worsen over time and may lead to chronic pain, nerve damage, or restricted movement. Early diagnosis and proper treatment help prevent complications and support long-term spine health.

When Do You Need Spinal Problem Treatment?

You may require spinal treatment if you experience:

  • Persistent or recurring back pain
  • Neck pain or stiffness
  • Slip disc or disc bulge
  • Sciatica or radiating leg pain
  • Tingling or numbness in arms or legs
  • Difficulty bending, standing, or walking
  • Posture-related spinal pain
  • Pain after injury or accident
  • Muscle weakness linked to nerve pressure
  • Reduced flexibility or spinal stiffness

Spinal problem treatment is ideal for those seeking non-surgical and drug-free solutions for long-term relief.

Can spinal treatment be combined with other therapies?
