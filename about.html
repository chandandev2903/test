Introduction

At Navtej Center, our mantra is “No Pain, No Intake of Medicines, No Side Effects” — reflecting our commitment to drug-free, non-invasive therapies that treat the root cause, not just the symptoms. Every treatment plan is personalized, ensuring lasting relief and improved overall wellness.

At Navtej Acupuncture, Chiropractic & Cancer Nutrition Center, we believe in healing naturally. Led by Mr. Naveen Kumar. L, MPharm., D.Acu., D.Ch.T & Onco Nutritionist, Principal of Sarvodaya College of Pharmacy, Manvi, our center is renowned in Manvi, Raichur, Sindhanur, and Sirwar for providing safe, effective, and personalized holistic therapies.

We specialize in Acupuncture (including cosmetic acupuncture), Chiropractic spine manipulation, Cancer Nutrition, Cupping therapy (Hijama), Seed Therapy, Magnetic Therapy, and Physiotherapy with G5 treatment. Our treatments target infertility, spinal problems (cervical, thoracic, lumbar), neurological conditions like migraine and paralysis, and pain affecting the neck, shoulders, back, knees, and scia

Cupping Therapy (Hijama)

Seed Therapy

Magnetic Therapy

A non-invasive therapy that applies magnetic fields to improve circulation, reduce nerve pain, accelerate healing, relieve joint and muscle discomfort, and enhance overall body function.

Cosmetic Acupuncture

A natural rejuvenation therapy that stimulates facial points, promotes collagen production, improves skin elasticity, reduces fine lines and wrinkles, and enhances complexion and vitality without chemicals or invasive procedures.
